### 📊 Tab 1: Recent Decks

Shows decks you've recently accessed, ordered by last use. This tab helps you quickly jump back into your active learning.

### 📁 Tab 2: All Decks

Your complete deck library with folder organization. Navigate through folders and subfolders to find any deck.

### 🌐 Tab 3: Explore

Community deck sharing (coming soon)

### 📰 Tab 4: Discover

Premium offers, updates, and community engagement.

**Features:**
- Premium feature highlights
- Feedback and suggestions
- App updates and news
