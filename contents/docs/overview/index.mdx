## Key Benefits

### ⚡ Intuitive Gesture Interface
- **Swipe** to navigate and delete cards
- **Pinch** to zoom content
- **Drag & drop** to reorder cards
- **Edge bars** show learning status and sync changes

### 🎨 Rich Content Support
HTML formatting, embedded YouTube videos, images, and bold text. Create engaging, multimedia flashcards that go beyond plain text.

### 💻 Code Syntax Highlighting
Perfect for developers: `<pre><code>` blocks support syntax highlighting for technical flashcards and programming study.

### 🔄 Bi-directional Sync with Excel/CSV
Edit flashcards on your computer using **Excel, Google Sheets, LibreOffice Calc**, or any CSV editor. Changes sync both ways—edit in the app or on your computer, everything stays in harmony.

### 📊 Never Lose Learning Progress
Your due dates, mastery levels, and learning statistics survive every sync. Update content without resetting your progress—the core feature that sets GoCards apart.

### 🖥️ Edit on Computer
Use **Excel, Google Sheets, LibreOffice Calc**, or any spreadsheet software to edit your flashcards. Faster typing on a full keyboard, better organization with spreadsheet tools—much more efficient than editing on your phone.

### 🖥️ Bulk Editing Made Easy
Find-replace across hundreds of cards, copy-paste entire sections, and use spreadsheet formulas. Edit hundreds of cards in minutes instead of hours.

### ☁️ Cloud Storage Integration
Save your synced file to Google Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive. Edit on your laptop, save, and changes appear on your phone automatically. No manual transfers.

### 🤝 Easy Sharing & Backup
Share decks as standard Excel/CSV files—no proprietary formats. Your content is always readable and backed up outside the app.

### 🎯 Spaced Repetition Learning
Smart scheduling shows you cards exactly when you're about to forget them, optimizing retention and minimizing study time.

### 🚫 No Ads
Clean, distraction-free learning experience. Focus on studying, not avoiding pop-ups.
