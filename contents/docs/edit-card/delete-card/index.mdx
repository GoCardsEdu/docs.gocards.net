## How to Delete a Card

GoCards provides multiple ways to delete cards, all with **undo support** so you never lose a card by accident.

### From Study Mode

1. While studying, tap the **🗑️ Delete** icon in the toolbar
2. The card is removed and the next card appears
3. Tap **"Restore"** in the snackbar or press **Back** to undo

### From Edit Mode

1. While editing a card, tap the **🗑️ Delete** icon
2. Card is removed immediately
3. Use the snackbar or **Back** button to restore

### From Cards List

**Method 1: Popup Menu**
1. Tap any card to open its menu
2. Select **"Delete"**
3. Card is removed with a snackbar showing **"Restore"** option

<center><img src="/images/docs/card-list-item-popup-menu.png" width="300" /></center>

**Method 2: Swipe to Delete**
1. Swipe any card **left or right**
2. Card is deleted instantly
3. Tap **"Restore"** in the snackbar to undo

**Method 3: Bulk Delete**
1. Long-press a card to enter selection mode
2. Tap additional cards to select them
3. Tap the **Delete** icon in the toolbar
4. All selected cards are removed with undo option

<center><img src="/images/docs/card-list-item-select-popup-menu.png" width="300" /></center>

## Undo Options

GoCards uses an **undo-first approach** instead of confirmation dialogs, making deletion quick while still safe:

| Method | How to Undo |
|--------|-------------|
| **Snackbar** | Tap "Restore" in the message that appears after deletion |
| **Back button** | Press back immediately after deleting (Study/Edit mode) |

**Note:** The undo option is available for a short time after deletion. Act quickly if you need to restore a card.

---

## Tips

- **Swipe is fastest** - In Cards List, swipe left or right for quick deletion
- **Bulk delete** - Long-press to select multiple cards, then delete all at once
