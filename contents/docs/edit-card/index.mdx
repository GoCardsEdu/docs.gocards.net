## How to Access the Edit Screen

You can edit cards from three locations:

**1. From Cards List**

Tap any card and "Edit" from the popup menu.

<center><img src="/images/docs/card-list-item-popup-menu.png" width="300" /></center>

**2. From Study Mode**

Tap the "Edit" from the top app bar

**3. From Browse Mode** (Premium feature)

Tap the "Edit" from the top app bar

---

## Topbar Actions

On the topbar, you can see the following actions:

- **💾 Save** - Save your changes to the card
- **🗑️ [Delete](/edit-card/delete-card)** - Remove this card from your deck
- **⋮ More menu:**
    - **[Create new card](/edit-card/add-card)** - Add another card to this deck


**Note:** Your learning progress (review history, due dates, intervals) remains unchanged when you edit a card.
