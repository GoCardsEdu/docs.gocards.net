## How to Add a New Card

You can add cards from several places in the app:

### From Cards List

**Method 1: Add at the end of deck**
1. Open your deck's Cards List (tap "⋮" on a deck → "List Cards")
2. Tap the **"+" button** in the top toolbar
3. Enter your card content and tap **Save**

**Method 2: Insert at a specific position**
1. In Cards List, tap any card to open its menu
2. Select **"Add a new card here"**
3. The new card will be inserted after the selected card

### From Study Mode or Edit Card Screen

1. Tap the **"⋮" menu** in the top toolbar
2. Select **"New card"**
3. Enter your content and save

---

## Card Fields

When creating a new card, you'll see two main text fields:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Term** (Front) | The question, word, or prompt you want to memorize |
| **Definition** (Back) | The answer, meaning, or information to recall |

Both fields support multiple lines and [HTML formatting](/docs/edit-card/html-tags/) for rich content like bold text, images, and links.

---

## Toolbar Actions

While adding a card, the toolbar provides:

- **💾 Save** - Save the new card to your deck
- **🗑️ Delete** - Discard the card without saving
- **⋮ More menu:**
    - **New card** - Save current card and start another new card

## Tips for Creating Cards

- **Keep it simple** - One concept per card works best for memorization
- **Use both sides** - Put the prompt on the front, the answer on the back
- **Add context** - Include example sentences for vocabulary cards
- **Format when needed** - Use [HTML tags](/docs/edit-card/html-tags/) for emphasis or images
