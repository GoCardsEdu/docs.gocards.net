The Cards List screen is your deck management hub. View all cards at once, search, reorder, edit, delete, and perform bulk operations.

## Opening Cards List

From the home screen:

1. **Tap on a deck** to open deck menu
2. **Select "List Cards"**

You'll see all your cards in a scrollable list/grid view.

- **Card number** (ordinal position)
- **Term** (front of card) - bold text
- **Definition** (back of card) - preview truncated if long
- **Max lines** - Configurable in [Deck Settings](/docs/settings/deck-settings/)

## Searching Cards

### How to Search

1. **Tap the ğŸ” search icon** (top-right)
2. **Type your query** in the search field
3. **Results filter instantly** as you type
4. **Tap X** to clear search

### Search Features

- **Searches both** term and definition
- **Case-insensitive** - "DNA" matches "dna"
- **Partial matches** - "proto" matches "protocol" and "protein"
- **Real-time** - Updates as you type
- **Highlights matches** (if supported)

### Tips for Searching

- **Find duplicates** - Search for common terms
- **Locate cards to edit** - Search then tap to edit
- **Review specific topics** - Search for keywords
- **Verify imports** - Search for terms you know should exist

## Quick Card Actions

### Tap a Card

Opens the card's **action menu:**

- **Edit Card** - Modify term/definition (see [Editing Cards](/docs/cards-list/editing/))
- **Delete Card** - Remove this card
- **Select Card** - Enter multi-select mode (see [Multi-Select](/docs/cards-list/multi-select/))
- **Add Card After** - Insert a new card at this position

### Long-Press a Card

Opens the same action menu as tapping.

### Swipe a Card (Left or Right)

**Swipe to delete** - Card disappears with undo option

See [Deleting Cards](/docs/cards-list/deleting/) for details.

## Adding New Cards

### Add at End of Deck

1. **Tap "+" button** (top-right)
2. **Enter term and definition**
3. **Tap save icon** (ğŸ’¾)

Card appears at the bottom of the list.

### Add After Specific Card

1. **Tap a card** to open menu
2. **Select "Add Card After"**
3. **Enter term and definition**
4. **Save**

New card appears immediately after the selected card.

### Bulk Add

For adding many cards:
- Use [Excel/CSV Import](/docs/sync-export-import/import-cards/)
- Edit in spreadsheet software (easier for bulk entry)
- Import or sync to GoCards

## Understanding Card Numbers

Each card has an **ordinal number** (1, 2, 3, ...) showing its position:

### Position Matters
- **Study mode** shows cards in this order (for new cards)
- **Browse mode** follows this sequence
- **Export to Excel/CSV** preserves this order

### Changing Numbers
- **Reorder cards** - Numbers update automatically
- **Delete a card** - Subsequent cards renumber
- **Add a card** - Gets next available number (or inserted position)

### Tips
- **Group related cards** - Keep similar topics together
- **Difficulty progression** - Easy â†’ Hard or vice versa
- **Logical sequence** - Follow a learning path

## Edge Bar Details

### Left vs. Right Edge Bar

Configurable in [App Settings](/docs/settings/app-settings/):
- **Show left edge bar** - Status appears on left side
- **Show right edge bar** - Status appears on right side
- **Show both** - Redundant but highly visible
- **Hide both** - Clean interface, no status indicators

### Status Priority

If a card has multiple statuses, the edge bar shows:
1. **Sync status** (if recently synced) - Orange/Green
2. **Forgotten status** (if marked "Again") - Red
3. **Learning status** (if being studied) - Yellow/Green
4. **Disabled status** (if disabled) - Gray

### After Syncing

Edge bars help you see what changed:

**Scenario:** You edited 5 cards in Excel and synced.

**In Cards List:**
- 5 cards have **bright orange edge bars** (updated from file)
- Other cards unchanged

This visual feedback confirms sync worked correctly.

## Tips for Managing Large Decks

### Use Search Extensively
- Don't scroll through 500 cards
- Search for what you need
- Much faster than visual scanning

### Regular Maintenance
- Delete duplicate cards
- Fix typos as you find them
- Keep definitions concise

### Organize Before Import
- Structure your Excel/CSV file well
- Cards import in the order they appear
- Easier to organize in spreadsheet software

### Monitor Edge Bars
- Forgotten cards (red) need attention
- Consider editing to make clearer
- Or study more frequently

### Disable Instead of Delete
- Card not relevant now but might be later?
- Disable it (won't show in study mode)
- Re-enable when needed

*(Note: Disable feature may require specific implementation - check if available)*

## Common Workflows

### Review and Edit New Cards

After bulk import:

1. Open **Cards List**
2. Scroll through cards
3. Tap any card to edit
4. Fix formatting, typos, clarity
5. Repeat until satisfied

### Find and Fix Duplicates

1. **Search for common terms**
2. If you see duplicates, decide which to keep
3. **Edit one** to merge information
4. **Delete the other**

### Reorganize Deck Structure

1. Decide on logical order (alphabetical, difficulty, topic)
2. **Long-press and drag** cards to reorder (see [Reordering](/docs/cards-list/reordering/))
3. Save time by organizing in Excel first, then syncing

### Prepare for Exam

1. **Search for relevant topics** (e.g., "Chapter 5")
2. **Review those cards** in Browse Mode or study them
3. **Edit** any confusing cards for clarity
4. **Study mode** to drill the deck
