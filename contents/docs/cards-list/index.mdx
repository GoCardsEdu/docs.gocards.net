### Opening Cards List

From the home screen:

1. **Tap "⋮" on a deck** to open the deck menu.
2. **Select "List Cards"**

You'll see all your cards in a scrollable list/grid view.

---

### Card List Display

Each card shows:
- **#** - Card number, ordinal position
- **Term** - front of card
- **Definition** - back of card
- **Edge Bars** (left or right) - Colored bars indicate card status
- **Max lines** - Configurable in [Deck Settings](/docs/settings/deck-settings/)

## Card Actions

### Tap a Card

**Tap a Card** opens the card's **action menu:**

- **✏️ [Edit](/edit-card)** - Modify term/definition
- **🗑️ [Delete](/edit-card/delete-card)** - Remove this card
- **✂️ [Select / Cut](/cards-list/multi-select)** - Enter multi-select mode
- **➕ [Add a new card here](/edit-card/add-card)** - Insert a new card after this position

<center><img src="/images/docs/card-list-item-popup-menu.png" width="300" /></center>

### Long-Press a Card

**Long-Press a Card** select the card and switch to the multi-select mode.

See [Multi-Select Cards](/cards-list/multi-select) for details.

### Swipe a Card (Left or Right)

**Swipe to delete** - Card disappears with undo option

See [Deleting Cards](/docs/cards-list/deleting/) for details.
