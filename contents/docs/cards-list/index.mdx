### Opening Cards List

From the home screen:

1. **Tap "⋮" on a deck** to open the deck menu.
2. **Select "List Cards"**

You'll see all your cards in a scrollable list/grid view.

---

### Card List Display

Each card shows:
- **#** - Card number, ordinal position
- **Term** - front of card
- **Definition** - back of card
- **Edge Bars** (left or right) - Colored bars indicate card status
- **Max lines** - Configurable in [Deck Settings](/docs/settings/deck-settings/)

---

### Toolbar Actions

- **🔍 Search** (Premium feature)- Filter cards by term or definition text
- **+ Add Card** - Add a new card at the of deck
- **⋮ More Menu:**
    - Sync with Excel/CSV
    - Export to Excel
    - Export to CSV
    - ⚙ Settings

---


## Card Actions

### Tap a Card

**Tap a Card** opens the card's **action menu:**

- **Edit** - Modify term/definition (see [Editing Cards](/docs/cards-list/editing/))
- **Delete** - Remove this card
- **Select / Cut** - Enter multi-select mode (see [Multi-Select](/docs/cards-list/multi-select/))
- **Add a new card here** - Insert a new card after this position
