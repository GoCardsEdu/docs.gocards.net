title: Edge Bars (Left or Right)
description: Visual indicators on the left or right side of cards that show learning progress or recent sync activity.
keywords: []
---

### Show learning status

Shows your memorization progress for each card:

- **⚪ Gray** - Disabled card (won't appear in study mode)
- **🟠 Orange** - Forgotten card
- **🟢 Green** - Memorized card

### Show recently synced

Shows which cards changed during the last sync with your Excel or CSV file:

#### From File to App
- **🟠 Bright Orange** - Card was updated from the file
- **🟢 Bright Green** - Card was added from the file

#### From App to File
- **🟠 Dim Orange** - Card was updated from the app
- **🟢 Dim Green** - Card was added from the app

#### Unchanged
- **No color** - Card wasn't modified during sync

Learn more about how sync works in [Sync Cards](/docs/sync-export-import/sync-cards).

### Changing Edge Bar Settings

Configure edge bar visibility in [App Settings](/docs/settings/all-decks#show-left-edge-bar).
