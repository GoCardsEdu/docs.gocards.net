The ← (back button) has two different functions depending on where you tap it:

**1. Exit current activity** (top bar)
- **Tap ←** in the top bar to close your current activity and return to the Deck List

**2. Undo last action** (bottom navigation bar)
- **Tap ←** in the bottom navigation bar to reverse your most recent operation

### **What you can undo:**

When you tap ← in the bottom navigation bar, you can:
- **Undo grading** – Return to the previous card after selecting Easy, Good, Hard, or Again
- **Restore deleted card** – Recover a card you just removed
- **Exit Edit Card mode** – Return to your previous activity (Study or Browse mode)
