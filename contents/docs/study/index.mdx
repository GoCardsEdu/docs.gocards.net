Study Mode is where the learning happens! GoCards uses **spaced repetition** to help you memorize efficiently by showing cards at optimal intervals.

## Starting a Study Session

From the home screen:

1. **Tap on a deck**
2. **Select "Study Cards"**
3. **Your first card appears**

GoCards automatically selects which cards to show based on:
- Cards you've never seen before
- Cards due for review today
- Cards you marked as "Again" recently

## Interactive Gestures

Study mode is highly interactive. No need to dig through settings!

### Pinch to Zoom

**Gesture:** Pinch out (spread fingers) or pinch in on any card section

**What it does:**
- Increases or decreases text size
- Applies to both term and definition
- Zoom level is saved per deck

**Great for:**
- Reading small text
- Fitting more content on screen
- Accessibility needs

### Adjust Term/Definition Ratio

**Gesture:** Drag the horizontal bar between term and definition

**What it does:**
- Makes term section larger/smaller
- Makes definition section smaller/larger
- Ratio is saved per deck

**Useful when:**
- Your terms are short but definitions are long (or vice versa)
- You want to see more of one section
- Different decks have different content lengths

### Reset View

**What it does:**
- Restores default term/definition ratio

## Understanding Review Intervals

The interval shown below each button is **when you'll see this card again**.

### How Intervals Work

**New cards** (never studied):
- Again: 2 days
- Quick: 5 minutes
- Hard: 3 days
- Easy: 5 days

**Previously studied cards:**
- Intervals adapt based on your history
- More "Easy" clicks = longer intervals
- "Again" clicks = shorter intervals
- The algorithm balances memory retention with study efficiency

### Why These Intervals?

GoCards uses **spaced repetition** based on memory research:
- Review just before you're about to forget
- Longer intervals strengthen long-term memory
- Too frequent = waste of time
- Too infrequent = you'll forget

## Study Session Flow

### When Cards Appear

1. **New cards** - Never studied before
2. **Due cards** - Review interval has passed
3. **Forgotten cards** - Recently marked "Again"

### Session Length

A study session includes:
- All due cards (scheduled for today or earlier)
- Up to 20 new cards (configurable)
- "Again" cards loop back into current session

You can study as long as you want. The session ends when:
- No more cards are due
- You've studied all new cards
- You press the back button

### "No More Cards" Screen

When you've finished:
- Congratulations message appears
- Shows how many cards you studied
- Option to continue browsing all cards
- Back button returns to deck menu

## Tips for Effective Studying

### Be Honest with Yourself
- Don't mark "Easy" if you hesitated
- Use "Again" freely - it helps you learn
- The algorithm works best with accurate feedback

### Consistency Over Duration
- **Better:** 10 minutes daily
- **Worse:** 70 minutes once a week
- Spaced repetition requires regular reviews

### Use "Quick" Strategically
- Great for cramming before tests
- Don't use as default - defeats spaced repetition
- Switch to Hard/Easy for long-term learning

### Don't Skip "Again" Cards
- These are your weakest cards
- Will reappear in current session
- Master them before moving on

### Adjust Settings
- Increase forgotten card limit if sessions are too short
- Decrease if you're overwhelmed
- See [Deck Settings](/docs/settings/deck-settings/)
