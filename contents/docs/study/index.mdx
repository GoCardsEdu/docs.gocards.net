## Starting a Study Session

GoCards automatically selects which cards to show based on:
- Cards you've never seen before
- Cards due for review today
- Cards you marked as "Again" recently

---

## Navigation

- **Swipe left/right** - Move between cards
- **Tap bottom section** - Reveal the definition (answer) and grading buttons

See all [Gestures](/docs/study/gestures/) including pinch-to-zoom and drag controls.

---

## Topbar Actions

- **✏️ [Edit Card](/docs/edit-card/)**
- **⋮ More Menu**
    - **+ [New](/docs/edit-card/add-card)** - Create new card
    - **🗑️ [Delete](/docs/edit-card/delete-card)** - Removes the current card (with confirmation)
    - **↩️ Reset view** - resets view settings like zoom, size, ratio betwen term and definition
