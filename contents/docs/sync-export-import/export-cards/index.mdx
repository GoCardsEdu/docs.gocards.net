Export your flashcard decks to Excel (.xlsx) or CSV (.csv) files to edit on your computer, share with others, or create backups.

1. **From home screen**, **tap on a deck** to open menu
2. **Select "More" (⋮)**
3. **Choose "Export to Excel"** or **"Export to CSV"**
4. **Choose save location**
5. **File is saved**
