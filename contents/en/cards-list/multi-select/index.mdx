## Entering Multi-Select Mode

**Method 1: Card Menu**
1. Tap a card
2. Select **"Select / Cut"** from the menu

**Method 2: Long-Press**
- Long-press any card to instantly enter multi-select mode

## Working with Selected Cards

### Selecting and Deselecting
- **Tap any card** to add it to your selection
- **Tap a selected card** again to deselect it
- Selected cards will be visually highlighted

### Multi-Select Actions Menu

While in multi-select mode, **long-press any card** to open the action menu:

- **Select** – Select subsequent cards in the list
- **Delete selected** – Permanently remove all selected cards
- **Paste** – Move all selected cards to appear after the long-pressed card

<center><img src="/images/docs/card-list-item-select-popup-menu.png" width="300" /></center>

### Exiting Multi-Select Mode

**Tap the back arrow (←)** in the toolbar to deselect all cards and return to normal browsing mode.

## Important Limitations

⚠️ **Deck-Specific Operations** – Cut and paste operations only work within the same deck. You cannot cut cards from one deck and paste them into a different deck.
