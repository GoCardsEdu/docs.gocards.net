### Card List Display

Each card shows:
- **#** - Card number, ordinal position
- **Term** - front of card
- **Definition** - back of card
- **Edge Bars** (left or right) - Colored bars indicate card status
- **Max lines** - Configurable in [Deck Settings](/en/settings/deck-specific#max-lines)

### Tap a Card

**Tap a Card** opens the card's **action menu:**

- **‚úèÔ∏è [Edit](/en/edit-card)** - Modify term/definition
- **üóëÔ∏è [Delete](/en/edit-card/delete-card)** - Remove this card

### Long-Press a Card

**Long-Press a Card** select the card and switch to the multi-select mode.

See [Multi-Select Cards](/en/cards-list/multi-select) for details.

### Swipe a Card (Left or Right)

**Swipe to delete** - Card disappears with undo option

See [Deleting Cards](/en/cards-list/delete-cards/) for details.
