## Deleting Single Cards

### Method 1: Swipe to Delete (Fastest)

**Swipe a card left or right** to quickly delete it.

### Method 2: Card Menu

**Tap a card** -> Select **"Delete"**

## Deleting Multiple Cards

1. **Long-press a card** to enter selection mode
2. Tap additional cards to add them to the selection
3. **Long-press a selected card** to open the popup menu
4. Tap **Delete selected**
5. All selected cards are removed, with an option to undo
