## How to Add a New Card

You can add cards from several places in the app:

### From Cards List

**Method 1: Add at the end of deck**
1. Open your deck's Cards List (tap "â‹®" on a deck â†’ "List Cards")
2. Tap the **"+" button** in the top toolbar
3. Enter your card content and tap **Save**

**Method 2: Insert at a specific position**
1. In Cards List, tap any card to open its menu
2. Select **"Add a new card here"**
3. The new card will be inserted after the selected card
