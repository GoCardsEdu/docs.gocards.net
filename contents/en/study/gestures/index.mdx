## Pinch to Zoom

**Gesture:** Pinch out (spread fingers) or pinch in on any card section

**What it does:**
- Increases or decreases text size
- Applies to both term and definition
- Zoom level is saved per card

## Adjust Term/Definition Ratio

**Gesture:** Drag the horizontal bar between term and definition

**What it does:**
- Makes term section larger/smaller
- Makes definition section smaller/larger
- Ratio is saved per card


## Reset View

**How:** Tap "⋮" menu → "Reset View"

**What it does:**
- Restores default term/definition ratio
- Resets text zoom to default
