## Your First Time Opening GoCards

Let's start in the **All Decks** tab.

<center><img src="/images/docs/all-decks-more-menu.png" width="300" /></center>

## Step 1: Create Your First Deck

[Decks](/en/deck-list) are collections of flashcards organized by topic. Let's create your first one:

1. **Tap the "All Decks" tab** at the bottom
2. **Tap the "+" button** in the bottom-right corner
3. **Enter a name** for your deck (e.g., "Spanish Vocabulary", "Biology Terms")
4. **Tap "Create"**

<center><img src="/images/docs/create-deck.png" width="300" /></center>

Your new deck now appears in the list!

## Step 3: Add Your First Cards

Now let's [add some cards](/en/edit-card/add-card) to your deck:

1. **Tap the "+" button** in the top-right corner
2. **Enter the Term** (the question - front of the card)
3. **Enter the Definition** (the answer - back of the card)
4. **Tap the save icon** (ðŸ’¾) to [save your card](/en/edit-card)

## Step 4: Study Your Cards

Now it's time to start your [study session](/en/study)!

1. **Go back** to your deck (tap the back arrow)
2. **Tap on the deck** to enter study mode

<center><img src="/images/docs/study-card-slider-more.png" width="300" /></center>

### Your First Study Session

In study mode, you'll see:
- **Term** at the top (the question)
- **Tap the bottom section** to reveal the definition (answer)
- **Swipe left or right** to navigate between cards

After revealing the answer, grade yourself based on how well you remembered:

| Button | When to Use | Next Review |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Again** (Red) | You forgot | Soon (stays in current session) |
| **Quick** (Orange) | For cramming | 5 minutes |
| **Hard** (Blue) | Remembered with difficulty | 3 days |
| **Easy** (Green) | Remembered easily | 5 days |

Each button shows when you'll see the card again. This is spaced repetitionâ€”the app schedules reviews right before you're likely to forget, making learning more efficient. If you prefer to review cards without affecting your study schedule, try [Browse Mode](/en/browse-cards).
