---
title: Creating a Deck
description: Organize related flashcards into decks.
keywords: ["create deck","new deck","add deck","deck creation"]
---

1. **Tap the "+" button** (top-right, available in Recent Decks or All Decks tabs)
2. **Enter deck name**
3. **Tap "OK"**

The deck appears in your All Decks list and will show in Recent Decks once you use it.
