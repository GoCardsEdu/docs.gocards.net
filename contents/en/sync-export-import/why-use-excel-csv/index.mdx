## Benefits of Using Excel/CSV

- ✅ Edit Cards on Your Computer
    - ✅ **Larger screen** - More comfortable than typing on your phone
    - ✅ **Bulk editing** - Use find-replace to update hundreds of cards instantly
    - ✅ **Edit Anywhere** - Update cards on laptop or phone in any program
    - ✅ **Faster data entry** - Create many cards quickly
    - ✅ **Better organization** - Arrange, categorize, and structure before importing
- ✅ Share Decks Easily
- ✅ Backup Content
    - ✅ **Cloud Storage Integration** - Works with Google Drive, Dropbox, and more


---

## Supports both Excel (.xlsx) and CSV (.csv) formats:

**Excel (.xlsx)**
- Compatible with: Excel, Google Sheets, LibreOffice

**CSV (.csv)**
- Best for: Universal compatibility, simple text editors
